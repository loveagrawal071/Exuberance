Exuberance-2K14

Exuberance-2K14 is a welcome party for the first year students of B. Tech which is organized by their immediate seniors.The evening mark's induction and integration of the new entrants in the J.K family. This year too, the Freshers’ Party of B. Tech students will be held, to synergize their efforts towards organizing and performing skills. Several group dances, skits, poetry and singing performances really make the new entrants enjoy to the fullest.The fresher's participate with full zeal & enthusiasm and display their cultural skills.On this cultural night full with fun and frolic panel of judges adjudge Mr. and Miss Fresher. In this get together party all the students get acquainted with each other and imbibe the rich tradition and culture of J.k Institute as well as contribute towards its achievement of excellence in all sphere of activities.
